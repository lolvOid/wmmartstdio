Biography

Name : Artist Win Myint Moe
Born : Nyaung Don , Ayerrawaddy Division, Myanmar
Studied Under : U Win Htein(Nyaung Don), U Thu Kha, U Thein Han, U Mya Aye and U Lun Gywe
Exhibitions : 2nd ASIAN ART SHOW, Fukuoka ( Japan ), Korea Painting with collection ( Myanmar and Korea ), Myanmar Fair on Santosa ( Singapore ,Malaysia and Thailand ), The World Water Color Triennal in Seoul ( Korea ).
Graduated : State School of Fine Art, Yangon, Myanmar
Major Medium : Oil Color, Acrylic Color, Watercolor
Gallery Membership : Artist Life Art Gallery, Hinnthar Art Gallery, Royal Hantha Art Gallery, Gallery 65
